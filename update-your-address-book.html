Update Your Address Book

In an effort to be more efficient as a family and as an homage to the patriarch of the Lewis clan, we have opted to project a more unified facade.

Enter Lewis Aviation

The past was a smattering of dark times rubbed across a canvas consisting of fibers with distinctly shoddy branding: @hotmail.com; @comcast.net; @united.net. It was a time when men were men and children were men, sharing the same adult subscriptions under the umbrella of a "unified inbox." Those times are no more.

Now, whenever you want to contact a member of the Lewis family, all you need to do is think of the name and affix @lewisaviation.com to the end of it (as you would a Forever stamp to a parcel). Do you have a hillarious video of a monkey falling down that you know Cynthia would get a kick out of? Try cynthia@lewisaviation.com. If you're feeling a tad less formal (the video involves him picking his nose — how gauche!), send it to cindy@lewisaviation.com. She'll still get it, in all its glory.

Don't try it, cool guy. I know you're smarter than us. There's no need to prove it by sending it to cici@lewisaviation.com, ceelo@lewisaviation.com, see-to-the-indy@lewisaviation.com or something equally ludicrious and complaining that the email bounced. These shortcuts are for your convienience. Just use what is listed below, smile and say, "Thank you."
